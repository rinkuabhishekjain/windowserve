Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Is the in-home consultation really free?

Yes — 100% free with no obligation. We come to your home, take a look at your windows, bring samples, and give you honest recommendations and a quote. There's never any pressure to buy.

How much do custom window treatments cost?

Cost depends on the product, size, and number of windows — but our pricing is always all-inclusive per window, covering the free consultation, professional measurement, and expert installation with no hidden fees. Call or text 515-WINDOWS for a custom quote.

Do you measure and install everything?

Absolutely. Every window is measured to the fraction of an inch and custom-ordered for an exact fit, then our team installs it cleanly and correctly. We treat your home with care and leave it spotless.

How long does the whole process take?

After your consultation and measurement, products are custom-made for your windows. Timelines vary by product and manufacturer, and we'll give you a clear estimate up front. We'll keep you updated and schedule installation as soon as your order arrives.

What products do you offer?

Blinds (faux wood, real wood, mini, vertical), shades (cellular, roller, roman, solar, blackout, motorized, pleated), plantation and specialty shutters, and custom drapery with hardware. See the full lineup on our products page.

Do you offer motorized and smart shades?

Yes. We offer app- and remote-controlled motorized shades compatible with Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit — perfect for hard-to-reach windows and smart homes.

Are your products covered by a warranty?

Our products come from premium manufacturers backed by their warranties, and we stand behind the quality of our installation. We'll walk you through the specific coverage for whatever you choose.

Which areas do you serve?

We provide in-home service throughout the Des Moines metro and central Iowa — including West Des Moines, Ankeny, Urbandale, Waukee, Clive, Johnston, Altoona, Ames, and more. Don't see your town? Call 515-WINDOWS; we likely cover it.

How do I get started?

Easy — call or text 515-WINDOWS (515-946-3697), email info@windowserve.com, or request a free quote online. We'll schedule your free in-home consultation at a time that works for you.
